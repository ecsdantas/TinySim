Welcome to TinySim!

TinySim is currently under development, which means it may have some bugs or malfunctions.

To get started, click on "Library" and drag some components onto the workspace. Connect the ports and run the simulation. Remember, all wires must be connected to ports; there are no connections allowed between wires.

General tips:
  • Delete items: Select the desired item and press [delete] or [del] on the keyboard.
  • Flip horizontally: Select the desired item and press [I] on the keyboard.
  • Configure block: Select the desired block and press [O] on the keyboard, or click on the respective gear icon.
  • Zoom: Rotate the scroll wheel.
  • Move item: Select the desired item, click and hold the mouse while dragging.
  • Pan: Click and hold on an empty area to move the entire circuit..

You can simulate up to 10000 steps. All simulation data and circuit are processed client-side, and we do not store any circuit on our servers.

TinySim is desktop only, and may do not work on mobile or tables.

To report bugs, ask for library or anything else: Google Forms for TinySim.
